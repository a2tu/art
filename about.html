One of my favorite early memories is painting with foam stamps in the tub during bathtime. It felt magical to dab a shape into a puddle of bright red, inky blue, or a daring bit of each before transferring the image onto the side of the tub.

I still love water soluble paint and remain devoted to color and distilled forms as ever, but I’ve expanded my inspiration from the bath to my surrounding natural landscape.


If you’re interested in my artworks, or have troubleshooting tips for painting on full-sized sheets (!), please say hello.
